Our Story

XoNote began in 2023 when our founder, Alex Chen, a security engineer, became frustrated with existing note-taking apps that compromised security for convenience.

After a security breach exposed his personal notes stored in a popular app, Alex decided to build a solution that would never compromise on security while maintaining simplicity and elegance.

What started as a personal project quickly grew as friends and colleagues recognized the need for a truly secure digital notebook. Today, our team is dedicated to helping people organize their digital lives without sacrificing privacy.

Our Mission

At XoNote, we believe that managing your digital life should be simple, secure, and stress-free. Our mission is to provide a unified platform where you can store and organize your most important information, from creative ideas to passwords, all in one secure environment.

We're passionate about helping people stay organized while maintaining complete privacy and security. We're building XoNote to be the last productivity app you'll ever need, with a focus on simplicity, security, and user experience.

Our goal is to empower individuals to take control of their digital information in a world where data privacy is increasingly important.

Core Values That Drive Us

We believe your data belongs to you. We design our systems with privacy as a foundation, not an afterthought. Your information never leaves your device without being encrypted.
