Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about XoNote features, account management, and more.

What is XoNote?

XoNote is a secure digital notebook that helps you organize your notes, tasks, and passwords in one place. It's designed to be simple, secure, and synchronized across your devices. With XoNote, you can create and manage notes, to-do lists, and securely store passwords with ease.

How do I get started with XoNote?

Getting started with XoNote is easy:

  1. Create a free account by clicking the "Sign Up" button at the top of this page
  2. Verify your email address through the confirmation link we'll send you
  3. Log in to access your dashboard
  4. Start creating notes, to-dos, or saving passwords by clicking the "+" button

The interface is intuitive and easy to navigate. You can switch between notes, to-dos, and passwords using the tabs at the top of your dashboard.

Is XoNote available on mobile devices?

Yes! XoNote is designed to work seamlessly across all devices. Our responsive web application works great on both desktop and mobile browsers. Additionally, you can install XoNote as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on your mobile device for an app-like experience. Just visit XoNote in your mobile browser and follow the prompts to add it to your home screen.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it by following these steps:

  1. Click on the "Login" button at the top of this page
  2. Click on the "Forgot Password?" link below the login form
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, please check your spam folder or contact our support team for assistance.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account at any time. To do so:

  1. Log in to your XoNote account
  2. Click on the settings icon in the top right corner
  3. Scroll down to the "Account Management" section
  4. Click on "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision by entering your password

Please note that account deletion is permanent and will remove all your data from our servers. Make sure to export or back up any important information before proceeding.

What's the difference between free and premium features?

XoNote offers both free and premium plans to suit different needs:

Free Plan:

  • Up to 5 notes
  • Up to 3 todo lists
  • 1 saved password
  • 1,000 character limit per note
  • Basic formatting options
  • Ad-supported experience

Premium Plan:

  • Unlimited notes
  • Unlimited todo lists
  • Unlimited passwords
  • No character limit for notes
  • Advanced formatting options
  • Ad-free experience
  • Priority customer support
  • Data export capabilities
  • Dark and light themes

You can upgrade to the premium plan at any time from your account settings.

Can I export my data from XoNote?

Yes, premium users can export their data from XoNote. To export:

  1. Go to your account settings by clicking the gear icon in the top right
  2. Navigate to the "Data Management" section
  3. Click on "Export Data"
  4. Choose what you want to export (notes, todos, passwords, or all)
  5. Select your preferred format (JSON, CSV, or PDF)
  6. Click "Export" to download your data

Please note that data export is a premium feature. Free users can upgrade to access this functionality.

How secure is my data in XoNote?

Security is our top priority at XoNote. We employ several measures to keep your data safe:

  • End-to-end encryption: Your data is encrypted before it leaves your device and can only be decrypted by you.
  • Secure authentication: We use industry-standard authentication practices and offer two-factor authentication for added security.
  • Data isolation: Your data is stored in isolated environments to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Regular security audits: We conduct regular security assessments and penetration testing to identify and address vulnerabilities.
  • Secure infrastructure: Our systems are hosted on secure cloud infrastructure with multiple layers of security controls.

We never store your master password in plaintext, and it's never sent to our servers. This ensures that even in the unlikely event of a data breach, your sensitive information remains protected.

How does the password manager work?

XoNote's password manager allows you to securely store and manage your passwords. Here's how it works:

  1. Your passwords are encrypted using AES-256 encryption, a military-grade encryption standard.
  2. The encryption is performed locally on your device before being stored in our database.
  3. To add a new password, simply click the "+" button while in the Password tab and fill in the details.
  4. You can view your stored passwords by clicking on them in your password list.
  5. For security, you'll need to re-enter your master password to view or edit sensitive password information.
  6. The password manager also includes a secure password generator to help you create strong, unique passwords.

With XoNote's password manager, you no longer need to remember multiple complex passwords or resort to insecure practices like using the same password for multiple sites.

Can I cancel my premium subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your premium subscription at any time. Here's how:

  1. Log in to your XoNote account
  2. Go to Settings by clicking the gear icon in the top right
  3. Navigate to the "Subscription" section
  4. Click on "Manage Subscription"
  5. Select "Cancel Subscription"
  6. Confirm your cancellation

When you cancel, you'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will revert to the free tier. We don't offer prorated refunds for partial months or years of service.

If you purchased a lifetime subscription, it does not require renewal and cannot be refunded after the 30-day satisfaction guarantee period.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee for all new premium subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days of your subscription, you can request a full refund by contacting our support team.

To request a refund:

  1. Contact support at support@xonote.com
  2. Include your account email and the reason for your refund request
  3. Our team will process your request within 3-5 business days

Please note that refunds are only available within the first 30 days of your initial purchase. Subsequent renewal payments are not eligible for refunds.
